# Monero General Fund Transparency Report - February 2025

**Note:** This is a preliminary report based on the [Reddit post](https://old.reddit.com/r/Monero/comments/1iavk5/monero_general_fund_transparency_report_february/). Following community discussion and any necessary clarifications, this report has been published on getmonero.org.

## Current General Fund Usage Policy

The general spending policy has remained consistent over the years. For reference, see the [previous report from March 2023](https://old.reddit.com/r/Monero/comments/11fslu9/monero_general_fund_transparency_report_march_2023/).

**In summary:**

1. **Infrastructure Costs**: The General Fund (GF) covers costs of basic infrastructure essential for the Monero project, including domains, hosting, CDN services, and other operational necessities.

2. **CCS Proposal Support**: The GF provides partial funding for Community Crowdfunding System (CCS) proposals deemed "fundamental" to the project. All donations to CCS proposals are immediately announced and can be tracked in real-time through comments on the [CCS repository](https://repo.getmonero.org/binaryFate).

3. **Policy Transparency**: Any changes to this spending policy would be communicated to the community immediately, without waiting for the next scheduled report.

## Reminder on Core Team Funding

Like all Core Team members, I am not and have never been compensated for my contributions to the Monero project. All Core Team work is done on a volunteer basis, driven by passion for the project and its mission.

## Current Wallet Setup

The General Fund structure has evolved to enhance security and operational efficiency:

### Historical Context
Until late 2023, we maintained:
- **GF**: The well-known public donation address (active for many years, accessible by myself, Fluffypony, and possibly other Core Team members)
- **GF2**: A wallet created when Fluffypony experienced legal issues in the US, designed to hold the bulk of funds

### Security Enhancement (End of 2023)
Following the CCS wallet security incident in late 2023, and despite the incident having no connection to my operations, I proactively revised my personal operational security setup. As part of this enhancement:
- **GF2** was deprecated and emptied
- All funds were transferred to a new wallet: **GF-Vault**

### Current Structure (as of February 2025)
- **GF**: Primary donation address (active)
- **GF-Vault**: Secure storage wallet for bulk funds
- **GF2**: Deprecated (balance: 0)
